About Me

I have always loved to create films ever since I was little. I started college as an undecided major. The summer of my freshman year to sophomore year, I had the opportunity to PA on the set of "The Hail Mary". I loved what I was doing on set and realized that this is what I wanted to go into. Since then, I have been a PA for several other companies and created projects that highlight my style and let me express my creative freedom.
